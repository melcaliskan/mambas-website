About us

A team with a different take on consultancy.

Mambas Consulting is a consultancy firm that believes the success of enterprise projects depends not only on methodology, but on standing beside the team.

With more than a decade of experience, we focus on three areas where we add the most value: project management consultancy, PMO & process consultancy, and data analytics. We work with a broad toolkit — from classical project management to agile methodologies (Agile, Scrum, SAFe) — and adapt our approach to the unique dynamics of every engagement.

We value international partnerships: we are the official authorized reseller in Türkiye of DHTMLX, the globally recognized JavaScript UI component library. Our clients gain both direct access to licensed products and technical advisory for integrating these components into their enterprise systems.
